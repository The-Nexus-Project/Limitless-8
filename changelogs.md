<h1>v0.2.0</h1>

> ### **Quests return! New mods, QoL changes & some fixes!**

**Note: Pack is in its early days. Various issues, recipe conflicts and crashes etc are more common!**

**Please always do a fresh install when updating the pack between versions to avoid issues!** <br />
**Also remember to always backup your world(s) before updating!**


**Added**
- [FTB Quests](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/ftb-quests)
- [Iron Ender Chests](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/iron-ender-chests)
- [Just Dire Things](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/just-dire-things)
- [Polymorph](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/polymorph)
- [Polymorphic Energistics](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/polymorphic-energistics)


**Removed**
- [Trample Stopper](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/trample-stopper) <br />
  ^ (**Functionality exists in [Utilitarian](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/utilitarian).**)
- [Wider Ender Chests](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/trample-stopper) <br />
  ^ (**Replaced by: [Iron Ender Chests](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/iron-ender-chests).**)


**Notable Changes**
- **Disabled** the **login** chat **startup** & **warning messages** from **@Mekanism**.
- **Added "bent bread" recipes** to make **Bread craftable** in **2x2 grid**.
- **If** a **recipe conflict** exists you can now **choose output again**. <br />
  ^ (**If you do find any though, be sure to report them!**)
- **@Crafting on a Stick** now **once again** has **@Curios support**.
- **Modified** the **loading screen background** a **little bit**.
- **You can now zoom** while **holding down X** by **default**. <br />
  ^ (**Use scroll wheel to zoom in or out further**.)
- **Re-added** the **welcome advancement**.
- (**!**) **Changes** in **mod updates not noted here**.


**Notable Fixes**
- **Fixed** some **custom datapack** related stuff **not working correctly**.
- **Many uncraftable items** from **various mods** has been **fixed**.
- (**!**) Some **fixes** not **noted** in **mod updates**, as **always**.


**Quests**
- **Re-added 2 Chapters** under the **"Magical Essence" category**. <br />
  ^ (**@Occultism & @Relics**.)
- **Re-added 5 chapters** under the **"Technology" category**. <br />
  ^ (**@AE2, @Gadgets, @Iron Galore, @Mekanism & @Powah**.)
- **Re-added 5 Chapters** under the **"Vanilla Plus" category**. <br />
  ^ (**@Minecraft, @Adventure, @Husbandry, @The Nether & @The End.**)
- **In total 12 chapters with 653 quests have been added**.
- **Note: Due to breaking changes in FTB Quests, all quests & chapters will need some extra work to get working.** <br />
  **Stay tuned for more.**


**Updated**
- (**!**) All **mods** that **could** be **updated** have been **updated**.
