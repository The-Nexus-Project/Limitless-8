<h1>v0.4.0</h1>

> ### **New mods. Lots of changes, fixes & more!**

**Note: Pack is in its early days. Various issues, recipe conflicts and crashes etc are more common!**

**Please always do a fresh install when updating the pack between versions to avoid issues!** <br />
**Also remember to always backup your world(s) before updating!**

- [Thaumon](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/thaumon)
- [Uppers](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/uppers)


**Removed**
- [Hunters Return](https://curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/huntersreturn) <br />
  ^ (**Due to worldgen crashes**.) <br />
  ^ (**Removed until it's possible to update NeoForge past 0.40**.)


**Notable Changes**
- **Removed unnecessary blocks** from the **Overworld Mining Dimension** such as **Gravel** & **Clay**.
- The "**Infinity**" Enchantment **no longer requires you** having **an arrow** in **your inventory**.
- **There's** now **no longer** a **Grass Block layer** ontop of the **Overworld Mining Dimension**.
- **Ores** in the **Mining Dimensions** now **generate at any Y level** like in **previous packs**.
- **Re-added** the "**Mysterious Bundles**" & "**Time is infinite**" advancements.
- **There's now** a **small warmup timer** before you **teleport** with **commands**. <br />
  ^ (**Commands such as /rtp, /tpa, etc**.)
- **You can now activate** a **dark theme** in **GUIs** if **you prefer that**.
- **You can now use trims**, **dyes** & **banner patterns** on **Elytra(s)**.
- **Disabled Double Door opening** for **Fence Gates** & **Trapdoors**.
- **Adjusted recipes** for the **Mining Dimension Access Blocks**.
- **Using /rtp** will now **land you further away** in **distance**.
- **Removed** the **sorting button** from **some GUIs**.
- (**!**) **Changes** in **mod updates not noted here**.


**Notable Fixes**
- **Market block** not **dropping itself** when **harvested** is now **fixed**.
- "**Fixed**" a **worldgen crash** related to "**ArrowEntity**".
- **Armadillos not spawning** is now **fixed**.
- (**!**) Some **fixes** not **noted** in **mod updates**, as **always**.


**Quests**
- **Adjusted some XP rewards** in the **@Iron Galore chapter**.
- **Minor fixes**.


**Updated**
- (**!**) All **mods** that **could** be **updated** have been **updated**.


**Current NeoForge Version**
- 21.0.30-beta


**Notes**
- Expect **breaking updates**, **weirdness**, **crashes** & **issues**, **bugs**, **recipe conflicts** & **overall instability**.
